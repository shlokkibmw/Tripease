Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change or cancel my booking?
To change or cancel your booking, go to the "My Trips" section, select the booking you want to modify, and follow the instructions. Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the provider and fare type.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, bank transfers. Payment options may vary based on your country.
How long does it take to receive a refund?
Refunds typically take 7-10 business days to process, depending on your bank or payment method. The exact timing may vary based on the provider's policies.
Can I make changes to my booking after check-in?
Changes after check-in are generally not possible for flights. For hotels, you may be able to extend your stay subject to availability. Please contact customer support for assistance.
What should I do if my flight is cancelled?
If your flight is cancelled, we will automatically notify you via email and SMS. You can then choose to be rebooked on the next available flight or receive a full refund. You can also contact our 24/7 support line for immediate assistance.
